Tools of Titans: The Tactics, Routines, and Habits of Billionaires, Icons, and World-Class Performers (2016) is a self-help book by Timothy Ferriss, an American writer, educational activist, and entrepreneur.[2] He interviewed more than 100 "world class performers" in diverse fields on their advice for success. The book debuted as a #1 New York Times bestseller.[3]
Arnold Schwarzenegger wrote the foreword.[4]

Content[edit]
Ferriss says he noticed common patterns in guests:[5]

80% of guests had a daily meditation or mindfulness practice[6]
A number of males (not females) over 45 never eat breakfast, or eat only the scantiest of fare.
Many use the ChiliPad device.[7]
Many enjoyed the books Sapiens, Poor Charlie's Almanack, Influence, Man's Search for Meaning.
Listen to single songs on repeat for focus.
A form of "spec" work (completing projects on their own time and dime, then submitting them to prospective buyers).
The belief that "failure is not durable".
Turned a weakness into an advantage.
Featured guests[edit]
Ferriss divides the guests' chapters into three categories: Healthy, Wealthy, Wise.[8]
