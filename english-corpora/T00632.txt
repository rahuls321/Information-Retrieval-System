RK Beach also known as Ramakrishna Beach is situated on the east coast of Bay of Bengal in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh.[1] It is located near Dolphin's Nose.[2]
RK Beach gets its name from the Ramakrishna Mission ashram situated near the beach.
