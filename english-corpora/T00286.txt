 Ayyampuzha  is a village and panchayat in Angamaly  Ernakulam district in the Indian state of Kerala.  Kalady plantations is a part of the panchaayat.[1]

Tourism[edit]
Ayyampuzha shares the famous Athirappilly Falls with Athirappilly in Thrissur.

Demographics[edit]
As of 2001[update] India census, Ayyampuzha had a population of 14,902 with 7665 males and 7237 females.[1]
