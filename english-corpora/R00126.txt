Kalaberake is a 2017 Indian Kannada-language film directed by Madhu Diwakar starring Anirudh and Sanjana Prakash in the lead roles.


== Cast ==
Anirudh
Sanjana Prakash as Nandini
Jai Jagadish
Bank Janardhan


== Soundtrack ==
Keerthi Jain composed the music for the film and the soundtracks, with lyrics for the soundtracks penned by Jain, Jithendra, Madhu Diwakar and Prasanna. The album has five soundtracks.
