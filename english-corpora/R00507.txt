The Issaquah Sportsmen's Club is a historic building in Issaquah, Washington.


== Description and history ==
The building's address is 23600 SE Evans Street. It was built in 1937 as part of the Works Progress Administration (WPA) with in the WPA Rustic architectural style. It is a one-story 40 by 32.3 feet (12.2 by 9.8 m) building constructed of vertical half logs about 8 inches (200 mm) wide. The building was moved from a similar wooded site to its current location in 1993. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places on November 19, 1998.
