Issues of substantial disagreement among scientists
This article relies largely or entirely on a single source. Relevant discussion may be found on the talk page. Please help improve this article by introducing  citations to additional sources.Find sources: "Scientific controversy" – news · newspapers · books · scholar · JSTOR  (September 2017)
A scientific controversy is a substantial disagreement among scientists. A scientific controversy may involve issues such as the interpretation of data, which ideas are most supported by evidence, and which ideas are most worth pursuing.
Controversies between scientific and non-scientific ideas are not within the realm of science and are not true scientific controversies.[1]
