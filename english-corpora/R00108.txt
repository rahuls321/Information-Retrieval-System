"Baby if," is Fayray's 9th single and last on Antinos Records. It was released on June 6, 2001 and peaked at #22. It was used as insert song in the TV drama "Ashita ga Aru sa" (in which she also starred). The coupling is a cover of Dorothy Fields's "Big Spender".
