Bijai Garh Fort is located at Bayana in Bharatpur District and 25km from nearest city Hindaun. The fort was built by Jadon Rajput King Bijai Pal in 1040 CE. 
Bijaigarh Fort contains several old temples and red stones pillars bearing an Inscription of Vishnuvardhan Feudatory of Samudragupta. The fort was described as one of the most famous forts in India by Babur himself.
