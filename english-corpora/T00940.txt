Ambolgad is a village near Jaitapur Nuclear Power Project in the Ratnagiri district in Maharashtra, India.
A Defence Officers Group named DARIYA (दरिया) has been formed by Col Dhanajirao Patil for bringing this village on World Map.[citation needed]
