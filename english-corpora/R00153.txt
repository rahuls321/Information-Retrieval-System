Branko Bajić (born 4 June 1998) is a Bosnian professional footballer who plays as a defender. He most recently played for Bosnian Premier League club Mladost Doboj Kakanj.


== Honours ==
Radnik Bijeljina
Bosnian Cup: 2015–16
