Episcopal Church of the Epiphany is a historic church located in New Iberia, Louisiana.
It is a one-story 34 by 74 feet (10 m Ã— 23 m) building which was built during 1857-58 of cypress timbers and bricks that were made by slaves.  Its side walls were reinforced by exterior buttresses in 1884, when a belfry was also added.  It was renovated in 1959.The church was listed on the National Register of Historic Places on April 29, 1977.
