Velyamikovo (Russian: Вельямиково) is a rural locality (a village) in Yugskoye Rural Settlement, Cherepovetsky District, Vologda Oblast, Russia. The population was 16 as of 2002. There are 3 streets.


== Geography ==
Velyamikovo is located 54 km southeast of Cherepovets (the district's administrative centre) by road. Kuznetsovo is the nearest rural locality.
