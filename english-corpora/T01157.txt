Manauli Fort is located in the Manauli village of Mohali district, Punjab, India. It is 11 kilometres (7 mi) from Mohali city and nearly 4 km away from International Chandigarh Airport Road Mohali. Manauli is a small village with a population of 3,919 and 693 households (2011 Census). As per land record area of the village is 738 hectares (1,820 acres) (s.no. 85).[1] The fort is 6.1 metres (20 ft) above the village habitation level.
