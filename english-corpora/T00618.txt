Sasihithlu beach also known as Daibittil beach[citation needed] is located in Sasihithlu village which is situated north of Mangalore city in the state of Karnataka in India.[1] It is situated approximately 6 km to the west of NH 66, and surrounded by the backwaters of Pavanje and Shambhavi rivers. Pavanje and Shambhavi rivers meet at this place. It is close to Mukka, one of the fastest developing localities of Mangalore.[1] This beach has hosted the Indian Open of Surfing in 2016[2] and 2017.[3]

Accessibility[edit]
Sasihithlu Beach is well connected by public transport. There are several city buses(2,2A) from the main bus stop in statebank and other parts of the city. One can also take the non-express service buses that give a stop for Sasihithlu Beach right at Mukka. Once off the bus, one can take auto to reach.
