Fenerbahçe University or FBU for short, is a private university in Istanbul established by the relevant foundation of the Fenerbahçe Sports Club.
At Fenerbahçe Faruk Ilgaz Facilities in April 2016; the workshop on its establishment, management and operation started.[3] In the first year of accepting students, Medicana Group invested ₺100M.[4] Security of the campus network and Internet of Things technologies were carried out with Aruba, a Hewlett Packard Enterprise company.[5] In 2020, it started to contribute to the National Combat Aircraft Project, which is carried out in cooperation with TÜBİTAK and TUSAŞ. Faculty of Communication students; In December 2019, started the social responsibility project "Neither Child Neither Adolescent"[6] and place in the Young Spokesperson of the Environment Competition organized by the International Environment Foundation in February 2020[7] won the first.
