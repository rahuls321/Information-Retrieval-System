Swini Nimesh Khara is an Indian child actress and a student at Narsee Monjee College of Commerce & Economics, Vile Parle, Mumbai. She is known for her role as the mischievous Chaitali in the TV show Baa Bahoo Aur Baby and for a role in the 2007 film Cheeni Kum. She also did an episodic role in CID as Shweta.
