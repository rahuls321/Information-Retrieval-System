Vachathi is a 2012 Tamil language film based on the Vachathi case. Kumari Talkies and Rethna Films jointly produced Vachaathi, written and directed by Ravithambi. Rethna Ramesh is the hero, and Tharushana, the heroine. Y. G. Mahendra, Pandu, Balu Anand, Nellai Siva and Kuyili form the supporting cast. Gurusiva has handled the camera, while Jack Vathsan has scored the music. C. Punniya has written the lyrics. S. P. Ahamed is in charge of editing. The story is based on the Vachathi case that took place years ago. DSP Jagannathan, the man who fought for justice in the case, and Shanmugham, president of the people's association in Vachathi, play important roles in the film. Director Ameer released the first audio CD, which was received by director S. P. Jananathan.[1]
