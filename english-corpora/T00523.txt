Bedni Bugyal is a Himalayan Alpine meadow and a hill station , situated at an elevation of 3,354 metres (11,004 ft) in the Chamoli district of Uttarakhand state of India. Bedni Bugyal falls on the way to Roopkund near Wan village.[1] Trisul and Nanda Ghunti are clearly visible from here. This lush green meadow is adorned with blooms in a wide range of varieties. There is a small lake named Vaitarani (Bedni Kund), situated amidst the meadow. The rich flora of the area includes  'Brahm Kamal' or Saussurea obvallata.
