Karjat (Pronunciation: [kəɾd͡zət̪]) is a town administered under a Municipal Council in Raigad district in the Indian state of Maharashtra. It is served by Karjat railway station. Karjat forms part of Mumbai Metropolitan Region. Karjat is located approximately at an equidistant of 100 km from Mumbai and Pune.The river ulhas flows through the town.
