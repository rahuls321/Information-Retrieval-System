Le Vivier-sur-Mer (Breton: Gwiver, Gallo: Le Vivier) is a commune in the Ille-et-Vilaine department of Brittany in north-western France.


== Population ==
Inhabitants of Le Vivier-sur-Mer are called in French vivarais.
