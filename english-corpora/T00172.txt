Chintoor or Chinturu is a mandal in East Godavari district of the Indian state Andhra Pradesh. It is located in Rampachodavaram revenue division.

Geography[edit]
Chinturu has an average elevation of 35 metres (115 ft). Geo Coordinates: 17° 44' N 81° 23' E. Chinturu is situated besides river Sabari. Sabari is sub river of Godavari river.
