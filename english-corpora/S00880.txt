The Code is an English-language Finnish documentary about Linux from 2001, featuring some of the most influential people of the free software movement.

Featured advocates[edit]
Free and Open-source advocates or programmers in the film:

Linus Torvalds
Richard Stallman
Alan Cox
Eric S. Raymond
Robert "Bob" Young
Jon "maddog" Hall
Theodore Y. "Ted" Ts'o
David S. Miller
Miguel de Icaza

Andrew Leonard
Larry Augustin
Martti Tienari
Sun Yu-Fang
Liang Changtai
Jay Salzenberg
