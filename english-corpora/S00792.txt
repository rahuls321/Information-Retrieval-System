In the English education system, science at GCSE level is studied through Biology, Chemistry and Physics.[1]

Single Award[edit]
Single Award Science has not been available to KS4 students in England since the reformed GCSEs first awarded in 2018 [2]

Double Award[edit]
Combined Science results in two GCSEs. Those with GCSEs in Combined Science can progress to A Levels in all of the three natural science subjects.
AQA offer two different specifications entitled Synergy and Trilogy.[3]

Triple Award[edit]
Triple Award Science results in three separate GCSEs in Biology, Chemistry and Physics and provide the broadest coverage of the main three science subjects.
The cqualifications are offered by the four main awarding bodies in the England; AQA, Edexcel, OCR, CIE and Eduqas.
