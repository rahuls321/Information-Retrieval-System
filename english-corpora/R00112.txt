Nettle Creek Township (T34N R6E) is one of seventeen townships in Grundy County, Illinois, USA.  As of the 2010 census, its population was 503 and it contained 187 housing units.


== Geography ==
According to the 2010 census, the township has a total area of 35.79 square miles (92.7 km2), of which 35.76 square miles (92.6 km2) (or 99.92%) is land and 0.03 square miles (0.078 km2) (or 0.08%) is water.
